For all of you who value yourself and your free time, the hotel Maja is the ideal place to spend quality time with your friends and family.

REASONS TO GO HOTEL*

Creative dining

We pride ourselves on our creative, pioneering dining concepts–from cafés to beach clubs. Our specialty restaurants are the talk of the town, and checking-in to our hotels is embarking on a unique culinary journey. Our chefs draw inspiration from cultures across the globe, distinct ingredients, and the latest trends to conceptualize vibrant and diverse dishes that delight the palate.

Minful wellness

We have our very own spa brand, LUX* Me, which includes a line of beauty products in partnership with renowned beauty gurus. But wellness goes far beyond the spa in our books. It's also a good night's sleep thanks to our pillow menu. And what you eat. All of our restaurants serve nourishing, plant-based dishes under the label Keen on Green. Plus, the destinations we are in are at the heart of nature, making for a breath of fresh air and some vitamin D.

Caring servise

Service is our passion. Always has been. We make our guests feel cared for by helping them celebrate life, and making each moment matter.

Conscious living

We care about what matters. To us, that's people–each other, guests, our community–and the planet we call home. We've noticed that today's travellers want to give in return. Through gestures big and small, we choose to live consciously. So far, we have embraced a Zero Food Waste lifestyle, reduced single-use plastic to near zero, helped protect sea turtles... It's a journey, and one we couldn't be happier to be on.

Extraordinary experiences

We launched a collection of one-of-a-kind Extraordinary Experiences that change the way you holiday. Curated by our team of travel experts, these experiences pull you into the heart of the destination you are in. Our Extraordinary Experiences are the textbook definition of "live a life well-travelled".
